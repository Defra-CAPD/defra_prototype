Your agricultural payments account

You can

  • view your business and land details
  • check and confirm your personal details

from January 2015 you can:

  • claim payments
  • manage your payments
  • apply for new funding

You will need

  • to register with GOV.UK to use this service
  • to have a Basic Payment Scheme online account
  • to be from England. The process is different if you are from Scotland, Northern Ireland and Wales
